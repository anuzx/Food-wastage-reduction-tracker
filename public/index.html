How Will You Get Benefitted !!

Your donation not only helps others but also rewards you!

1. Donate Food

Donate food through our platform. Whether you are an individual, restaurant, or NGO, you can easily contribute.

2. Earn Donation Coins

For every donation, you earn coins that can be redeemed for future contributions.

3. Receive Tax Receipt

Your donation comes with a tax receipt under section 80G of the Income Tax Act, India. This receipt allows you to claim tax deductions of up to 50% or more, depending on the charity's eligibility.
